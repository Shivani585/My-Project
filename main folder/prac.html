A.P.J Abdul Kalam

In 1997, Kalam received India's highest civilian honour, the Bharat Ratna, for his contribution to the scientific research and modernisation of defence technology in India.
In 2013, he was the recipient of the Von Braun Award from the National Space Society "to recognize excellence in the management and leadership of a space-related project".
Wheeler Island, a national missile test site in Odisha, was renamed Kalam Island in September 2015.
on February 2018, scientists from the Botanical Survey of India named a newly found plant species as Drypetes kalamii, in his honour.

Mahatma Gandhi

Father of India

Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi was an Indian lawyer, anti-colonial nationalist and political ethicist who employed nonviolent resistance to lead the successful campaign for India's independence from British rule. He inspired movements for civil rights and freedom across the world.
